About IDR

IDR is an industry-led institute that combines academic partnerships, real world deployment, and a mission to close the digital risk skills gap in regulated sectors. We empower students and professionals with training, frameworks, and project-based incubation.

We partner with universities, financial institutions, and critical infrastructure teams to make digital, cyber, and AI risk management accessible and actionable.

Service Pillars

Academy

Bootcamps, certifications and early-career tracks for cyber, cloud and AI risk professionals.

  • Risk Fundamentals
  • Hands-on labs, simulation projects
  • University-accredited modules

Innovation & Incubation

Developing IP, proof-of-concepts, and frameworks for AI governance and secure digital systems.

  • AI risk models
  • Secure product pipelines
  • Compliance-by-design workshops

Advisory

Implementation of NIST, ISO 27001, and sector-specific governance for regulated institutions.

  • Assessment & strategy
  • Controls & policy deployment
  • Managed transformation support

IDR’s core model: train → hire → innovate → deploy.

Community

We serve students, early-career professionals, and practitioners in financial services and critical infrastructure. Our community includes mentors, peer project teams, and industry sponsors.

Local and global networks support continuous learning through newsletters, workshops, and live risk scenario months.
